Big McLargeHuge

I was toying around with the idea of building a Twitter bot recently, to go along with my profile picture updater. At a time that was later than I should have been awake, I decided that programmatically generating nicknames for Dave Ryder of Mystery Science Theater 3000 fame would be a fun project.

Then I found out that SOMEONE ALREADY DID IT.

Either way, I had already completed most of the work:

  • Googling the above-linked list
  • Going to Thesaurus.com and trying to find similar word-chunks
  • Creating a very simple script to randomly combine words together into names

So I didn't put it up on Twitter, but here's my code. And a sampling of the names I generated that I found funny:

  • Flash FleshBlast!
  • Bulk McMeatSlab!
  • Buck VanderStrong!
  • Hunk FizzleChunk!
  • Peck SteakFlesh!
  • Wedge SpeedBeef!

...that's it
