What's This?

This little Twitter bot was inspired by the @everylotnyc Twitter profile, which posts Google streetview shots of all addresses in New York City that I stumbled across.

I'd been looking to build something on top of some Toronto open data, and this seemed like a good option.

How it do?

  • I used the Toronto One Address Repository to get a master list of addresses.
  • After some testing, it looked like searching for 
    {street number} {street name}, Toronto ON Canada
    would return the right spot from the streetview API.
  • From there it was just a matter of messing around with some of the settings on the API calls, setting it up to download the picture and metadata, and post to Twitter with Twython!
  • Then I scheduled it on my Raspberry Pi, after trying to remember how to get its IP address again

Why'd I do it?

I dunno, just messing about with Twitter I guess. Toronto has some neat houses in it though, and it's kind of delightful seeing them pop up in my Twitter feed a couple of times a day.

Notes

  • Code at toronto-streetview
  • Bot is live (as of 2017) at @EveryTOAddress
